# Realm of the Forgotten Crown — Player Handbook

## Getting Started

Welcome to the Realm! Connect with any telnet client to `realm.forgottencrown.net` port 4000.

When you connect, you'll be asked for a name. Choose wisely — it's how everyone will know you.

## Basic Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `look` (or `l`) | Look around the room |
| `north`, `south`, `east`, `west`, `up`, `down` | Move (or `n`, `s`, `e`, `w`, `u`, `d`) |
| `say <message>` | Talk to everyone in the room |
| `who` | See who's online |
| `inventory` (or `i`) | Check your inventory |
| `equip <item>` | Equip a weapon or armor |
| `attack <target>` | Start combat |
| `cast <spell>` | Cast a spell (if you know one) |
| `buy <item>` | Buy from a merchant |
| `sell <item>` | Sell to a merchant |
| `help` | Show this help |
| `quit` | Disconnect |

## Classes

| Class | Strength | Weakness |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Warrior** | High HP, heavy armor, melee damage | No magic, limited range |
| **Mage** | Powerful spells, area damage | Low HP, light armor |
| **Thief** | Critical hits, trap detection, stealth | Low HP, no heavy weapons |
| **Cleric** | Healing, buffs, decent armor | Lower damage output |
| **Ranger** | Ranged attacks, tracking | Jack of all trades |
| **Bard** | XP bonus songs, group buffs | Lower combat stats |

## Starting Zone: Thornwick Village

You begin at **The Shattered Tankard**, Thornwick's tavern. Talk to Bram the Innkeeper for advice. Visit the market to buy gear, the temple for healing, and the south gate for your first quest.

## Rules

1. **Be respectful.** Toxicity gets you banned.
2. **No exploiting bugs.** Report them to the team. We appreciate it.
3. **PvP is opt-in.** Only in designated PvP zones. No camping safe zone boundaries.
4. **No real-money trading.** Gold and items stay in-game.
5. **Have fun.** It's a text game in 2026. We're all here because we love it.
