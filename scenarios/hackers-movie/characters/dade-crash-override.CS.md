## Character Backstory

Dade Murphy was Zero Cool — the kid who crashed 1,507 systems in one day at age eleven, including the New York Stock Exchange. That stunt got him a federal conviction, a ban from touching computers or touch-tone phones until his eighteenth birthday, and a family that fell apart under the weight of it. Now he's eighteen, just moved to New York with his mother, new school, no friends. But the ban is over. He's back online with a new handle — Crash Override — and he's better than ever. He doesn't talk about what he can do. He just does it.
