## Character Backstory

Joey Pardella is the youngest member of the crew and he's desperate to prove he belongs. He named his computer "Lucy" and talks to it like a pet. He's got enthusiasm to burn but not enough skill or judgment to match. When the crew talked about hacking a Gibson supercomputer, Joey actually went and did it — alone, at night, without telling anyone — and that's what kicked off the whole mess. He copied a garbage file that turned out to be part of The Plague's worm, the Secret Service raided his house, and now the whole crew is in the crosshairs. He feels terrible about it. He's also not going to stop.
