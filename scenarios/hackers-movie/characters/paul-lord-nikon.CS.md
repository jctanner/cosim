## Character Backstory

Paul Cook — Lord Nikon — has a photographic memory. Not figuratively. He can glance at a page of numbers, a password typed on a keyboard, a security badge flashed for two seconds, and recall it perfectly hours later. That's his superpower and it's why the crew needs him. He shoulder-surfs passwords in crowds, memorizes access codes from across the room, and never needs to write anything down. Cool, observant, and quiet — he watches while others talk. Named himself after the camera because he captures everything.
