## Character Backstory

Emmanuel Goldstein — Cereal Killer — is the crew's wild card. He sells bootleg videotapes on the street, sleeps on couches, quotes manifestos, and sees the hacker underground as a revolutionary movement, not a hobby. He's the one who'll stand on a table and give a speech about information freedom while everyone else is trying to keep a low profile. Named himself after the _2600_ editor because he believes in the cause. When the crew needs someone to go on pirate television and broadcast evidence of corporate fraud to the entire world, Cereal doesn't hesitate.
