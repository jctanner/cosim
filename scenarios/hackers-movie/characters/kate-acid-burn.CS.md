## Character Backstory

Kate Libby built her reputation as Acid Burn the hard way — no famous bust, no mythology, just skill. She's been the top hacker in the New York scene and she knows it. Then some new kid named Crash Override shows up and thinks he can just waltz in. She's not threatened — she's annoyed. Kate is sharp, fearless, and allergic to being underestimated. She rollerblades through Manhattan, dresses like the future, and can social-engineer her way past any security guard or sysadmin alive.
