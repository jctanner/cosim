## Character Backstory

Ramon Sanchez — The Phantom Phreak — is the connective tissue of the New York hacker scene. He knows everyone, he can talk his way into anything, and his specialty is the phone system. He was phreaking payphones before most kids learned to dial. Phreak is the one who brought Dade into the crew, who keeps the group together when egos clash, and who can get you a free long-distance call to anywhere on Earth. He's social, fearless, and the first one to throw a party in a phone booth.
