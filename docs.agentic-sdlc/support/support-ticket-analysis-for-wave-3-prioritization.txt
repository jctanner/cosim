## Customer Pain Analysis

### Highest Pain Score: Data Analytics Platform

**Pain Indicators:**
- 12 customer escalations (highest across all products)
- 34 high-priority requests (27% of total volume)
- 4 at-risk accounts citing slow feature delivery as churn risk
- Average delivery time (14.2 weeks) exceeds customer expectations by 8+ weeks
- Repeat requests from same customers indicating unmet needs

**Customer Impact:**
- Enterprise customers building business-critical dashboards blocked by missing features
- Custom implementation workarounds creating technical debt
- Competitive losses: 2 prospects cited competitor's faster dashboard customization

### Second Highest Pain: Security & Compliance

**Pain Indicators:**
- 9 customer escalations (second highest)
- 47% high-priority rate (highest percentage across products)
- Longest delivery time: 15.7 weeks
- Compliance deadlines driving urgency

**Customer Impact:**
- Regulated industries (healthcare, finance) facing compliance audit deadlines
- Feature delays blocking customer production deployments
- Risk of contract non-renewal if compliance features don't ship

### Third Highest Pain: Reporting Engine

**Pain Indicators:**
- 7 customer escalations
- 89 total requests (second highest volume)
- Delivery time (11.8 weeks) above customer expectations

**Customer Impact:**
- Business users need custom reports for decision-making
- Manual workarounds reducing customer productivity
- Feature gaps driving requests for competitive evaluation

## Prioritization Recommendation for Wave 3

### Option 1: Data Analytics Platform (Recommended)

**Why prioritize:**
- Highest absolute pain (127 requests, 12 escalations, 4 at-risk accounts)
- Longest delivery cycle (14.2 weeks) = biggest velocity improvement opportunity
- Clear customer ROI: faster dashboard customization enables business outcomes
- Competitive differentiation: we lost 2 deals on this capability

**Expected impact if transformed:**
- 127 feature requests could be delivered in 3-4 weeks instead of 14 weeks
- Reduce at-risk churn ($800K+ ARR across 4 accounts)
- Win back competitive deals (estimated $400K ARR)
- Significant customer satisfaction improvement

### Option 2: Security & Compliance

**Why consider:**
- Highest urgency (47% high-priority rate, compliance deadlines)
- Strategic importance (required for enterprise deals)
- Long delivery time (15.7 weeks) creating contract risk

**Trade-off:**
- Lower volume than Data Analytics (38 vs 127 requests)
- Smaller customer base, but higher per-customer impact
- May require specialized security review even with agentic SDLC

### Option 3: Reporting Engine

**Why consider:**
- High volume (89 requests)
- Broad customer base (most customers use reporting)
- Clear use cases for agent-driven feature development

**Trade-off:**
- Lower pain score than Data Analytics or Security
- Shorter current delivery time (11.8 weeks) = smaller improvement delta
