## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

**WE ARE NOT COMPETING WITH RED HAT:**
- Red Hat provides Konflux as DIY component of RHEL/OpenShift
- We provide turnkey managed service with compliance focus
- Analogous to: Red Hat provides Linux, we provide "managed Linux hosting"
- Partnership potential: Referral agreements, reseller relationship

**COMPETITIVE DIFFERENTIATION:**
1. **Only managed Konflux offering** (first-mover advantage)
2. **Compliance-first** (FedRAMP, SOC2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS ready)
3. **No vendor lock-in** (Apache 2.0, customers can self-host)
4. **SLSA Level 3 native** (built-in supply chain security)
5. **Proven at scale** (2M+ artifacts in Red Hat production)

**VS. ALTERNATIVES:**
- GitHub Actions: No enterprise policy enforcement
- GitLab CI: Vendor lock-in, limited compliance features
- CircleCI: No built-in SLSA/provenance
- Jenkins: Complex maintenance, not Kubernetes-native
